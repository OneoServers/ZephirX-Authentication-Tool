Privacy Policy for Verify for Discord

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Verify for Discord ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following information:

  • Authentication Information: Roblox session token (.ROBLOSECURITY) to verify your Roblox account status.
  • User Profile Data: Username, display name, user ID, Robux balance, and avatar URL retrieved from Roblox's official API.
  • Web Activity: Limited information about whether you're currently on a Roblox domain to ensure the Extension functions properly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To verify your Roblox account for Discord integration
  • To transmit verification data to your designated Discord webhook
  • To provide the core functionality of the Extension

Data Storage and Security

The session token is only temporarily accessed and transmitted to your designated Discord webhook. We do not:

  • Store your data on our servers
  • Retain your data long-term
  • Access your data beyond the verification process
  • Collect any other cookies or personal information

Data Sharing

We only share your session token and verification data with your designated Discord webhook for the purpose of account verification. We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Transfer your data for unrelated purposes
  • Use your data for commercial purposes

User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • The Extension only collects data when you explicitly click the "Verify Account" button
  • You can remove the Extension at any time to stop data collection
  • You can clear your browser data to remove any stored information

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
